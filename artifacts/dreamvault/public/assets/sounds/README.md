Place reveal sound file(s) here. Recommended filenames:
- reveal-swell.mp3 (0.8-1.5s cinematic swell)
- reveal-whoosh.mp3 (0.08-0.3s whoosh)

You can download royalty-free SFX from freesound.org or use your preferred audio assets.

When adding the files, the RevealModal will automatically attempt to play `/assets/sounds/reveal-swell.mp3` on reveal.
